# Principles

#### Consistency

Our components are designed to provide a unified and consistent user experience.

#### Usability

We prioritize user-friendly designs that are intuitive and easy to use.

#### Adaptability

Components can adapt to various use-cases and screen sizes, providing flexibility in design.

#### Efficiency

Designs are optimized for performance, reducing load times and enhancing user satisfaction.

#### Accessibility

Every component is crafted to meet WCAG 2.1 AA and Section 508 requirements.

#### Modularity

Our architecture encourages modular and composable components.

#### Zero Styling Hassle

Save time and energy with our ready-to-use components. No need to mess with CSS or other styling languages.
